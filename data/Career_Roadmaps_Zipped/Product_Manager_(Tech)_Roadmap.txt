Career Roadmap: Product Manager (Tech)
========================================

Overview:
Product Manager (Tech)s work in their specialized field applying domain expertise and skills.

Key Skills:
- Domain-specific technical skills
- Problem-solving and analytical thinking
- Communication and teamwork

Key Subjects:
- Foundational subjects from the domain
- Applied knowledge for professional roles

Learning Path:
1. Learn the core concepts and principles of Product Manager (Tech)'s field
2. Gain hands-on experience through projects and internships
3. Acquire certifications or specialization courses relevant to Product Manager (Tech)
4. Contribute to professional networks and research communities
5. Advance into senior and leadership roles

Career Progression:
- Intern
- Junior Product Manager (Tech)
- Product Manager (Tech)
- Senior Product Manager (Tech)
- Lead / Consultant / Director
