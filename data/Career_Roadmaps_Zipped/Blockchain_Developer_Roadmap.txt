Career Roadmap: Blockchain Developer
========================================

Overview:
Blockchain Developers play a vital role in their domain, combining technical, analytical, and creative expertise to deliver impactful outcomes.

Key Skills:
- Mastery of core domain-specific tools and techniques
- Critical and analytical thinking
- Team collaboration and communication

Key Subjects:
- Foundational principles and theories of Blockchain Developer's field
- Applied studies in technology, management, or science as relevant

Learning Path:
1. Start by understanding the fundamentals of Blockchain Developer's field.
2. Build technical expertise through practice and projects.
3. Pursue internships, open-source, or professional experiences.
4. Specialize with certifications, higher studies, or industry training.
5. Move toward leadership and mentoring roles.

Career Progression:
- Intern
- Junior Blockchain Developer
- Blockchain Developer
- Senior Blockchain Developer
- Lead / Principal / Director
